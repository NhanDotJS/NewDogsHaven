Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is your pricing structure?
A: That depends on the breed of your furry friend, and the care plan you choose for them!
Q: What kind of activities do you offer for my dog?
A: We like to personalise our activities for each dog. Upon booking, we ask you to list a few of their favourite activities!
Q: What kind of vaccinations are required for my dog?
A: Your furry pal must meet all vaccination requirements that they would have to obtain to be taken out in public. Nothing out of the way!
Q: What happens if I am late picking up my dog?
A: We're all late sometimes. Just give us a call, and we'll happily look after your pal for a while longer!
Q: What kind of food and treats do you provide for my dog?
A: We like to personalise our diets and food plans for each dog. Upon booking, we ask you to list a few of their favourite foods!
Q: What happens if my dog gets sick or injured?
A: If your dog gets sick or injured, we will provide them with the necessary medical care. We will also contact you to let you know what happened and to get your permission for any necessary treatment.
Q: What happens if my dog gets into a fight with another dog?
A: If your dog gets into a fight with another dog, we will immediately separate the dogs and assess the situation. If either dog is injured, we will provide them with the necessary medical care. We will also contact you to let you know what happened and to get your permission for any necessary treatment.
Q: What happens if my dog is destructive or aggressive?
A: If your dog is destructive or aggressive, we will not be able to allow them to stay at our daycare. We understand that every dog is different, and we are committed to providing a safe and positive environment for all dogs.
