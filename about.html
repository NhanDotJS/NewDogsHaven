Daily Routine

Time Activity
8:00 AM Dogs arrive and are greeted by staff.
8:30 AM Breakfast is served.
9:00 AM Morning play begins. Dogs are divided into groups based on size, energy level, and temperament.
10:30 AM Morning break. Dogs are given a chance to rest and relax.
11:00 AM Morning play resumes.
12:30 PM Lunch is served.
1:00 PM Afternoon nap. Dogs are given a quiet place to rest and recharge.
2:00 PM Afternoon play begins.
3:30 PM Afternoon break. Dogs are given a chance to rest and relax.
4:00 PM Afternoon play resumes.
5:30 PM Dogs are picked up by their owners.
